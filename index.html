Best Practices & Recommendations
  • Aspect Ratio: 1:1 (square) recommended for optimal logo display
  • Resolution: Minimum 200×200px for reliable scanning across devices
  • Format: PNG with transparent background preferred, JPG/SVG also supported
  • Design: Simple, high-contrast logos work best for scanner recognition
  • Testing: Always test with multiple devices and QR scanner apps
